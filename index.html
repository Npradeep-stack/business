FAQ
How does the 40% advance work?
Pay 40% of the hall price online to confirm and lock your date. The remaining 60% is paid directly to the hall owner 2 days before your event day. No hidden charges.
What is the FREEDECOR100 coupon?
FREEDECOR100 gives you a free decoration consultation from our partner decorators plus an exclusive discount on their services. This is a partner benefit — not free decoration from the hall itself.
Can I pay the full amount upfront?
Yes! At checkout, choose "Option 2 — Pay Full Amount" to settle everything in one payment for complete peace of mind.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes. If you cancel, a 5% penalty on the advance and the 2% platform fee are deducted. The rest is refunded within 5–7 business days.
Is my payment secure?
All payments are processed via Razorpay — India's most trusted gateway — with 256-bit SSL, supporting UPI, Cards, Net Banking and Wallets.
